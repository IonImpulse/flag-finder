Flag Data

All flag data was collected manually, based off of schema found somewhere on the Internet.

Flag images link to Wikipedia when available for high quality SVGs, but otherwise are saved locally on GitHub. Country flags specifically are sourced from Flagpedia.

There are currently 1,456 flags in the database, in 7 data sets.
